Unicorn QR is a lightweight iOS app for generating clean QR codes for URLs and email links.

No accounts.  
No tracking.  
No clutter.

## Features

- Generate QR codes for:
  - Websites and URLs
  - Email links (mailto), with optional subject and body
- Instant generation with visual feedback
- Clean, distraction-free interface
- Works entirely on-device

## Privacy

Unicorn QR respects your privacy.

- No data collection
- No analytics
- No tracking
- No accounts
- No network services

For full details, see [PRIVACY.md](./PRIVACY.md).

## Platform

- iOS
- Built with SwiftUI
- Designed for iPhone
